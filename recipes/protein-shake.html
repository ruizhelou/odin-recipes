Protein Shake

Description

This is the ultimate pre-workout drink you will ever need. It has an extremely high protein to calorie ratio, and includes a lot of caffeine. This is not for kids and the taste is questionable. It also includes creatine.

Ingredients

  • Protein powder (1 scoop)
  • Instant coffee (2 tsp)
  • Creatine (~5mg)
  • V Blue Energy Drink Sugar Free (250ml)

Steps

  1. Add the protein powder, instant coffee, and creatine into a cup.
  2. Pour the V Blue Energy Drink into the cup.
  3. Stir until liquidy.
