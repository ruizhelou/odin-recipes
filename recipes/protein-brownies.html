Protein Brownies

Description

This is a high-protein and low-calorie recipe for brownies you can make at home. It is great if you want to cut down fat and build muscle, while enjoying the taste of a traditional brownie with a hint of banana.

Ingredients

  • Bananas x2 (~118g each)
  • Eggs x2 (~57g each)
  • Greek yoghurt (100g)
  • Protein powder (1 scoop)
  • Baking powder (2 tsp)
  • Cocoa powder (4 tbsp)
  • Monk fruit sugar (50g)

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 200 degrees celsius.
  2. Get a mixing bowl and mash bananas in the bowl until goey.
  3. Add greek yoghurt and eggs to the bowl, then whisk until liquidy.
  4. Add protein powder, baking powder, cocoa powder, and monk fruit sugar to the bowl, then whisk until no longer clumpy.
  5. Get a metal tray and coat it with a thin coating of butter.
  6. Pour the bowl into the mixing tray, then place the tray in the oven.
  7. Wait 30 minutes, remove the tray from the oven, then allow brownies to rest for 10 minutes.
